Game: tw04 — SymDraw (Symmetric Line-Drawing Puzzle)

Objective: Control the blue line from its start to its end; a yellow line moves symmetrically and must also reach its own end.

Movement:
- Actions 1-4 move YOUR (blue) cursor one step (UP/DOWN/LEFT/RIGHT).
- The yellow cursor moves automatically as a mirror of your move.
- Moving back retraces (undoes) the last step for both lines.

Symmetry types (varies by level):
- Horizontal mirror: your LEFT/RIGHT is flipped for yellow (UP/DOWN stays the same).
- Vertical mirror: your UP/DOWN is flipped for yellow (LEFT/RIGHT stays the same).
- Rotational (180°): both directions are flipped for yellow.

Constraints:
- Both lines must make valid moves (stay within the grid).
- The two lines cannot occupy the same node at the same time.
- Some edges may be broken (blocked) — neither line can cross them.
- If a move is invalid for either line, the move is rejected.

Submitting (Action 5 = CONFIRM):
- Blue line must be on the blue endpoint AND yellow line must be on the yellow endpoint.
- Both lines must pass through their respective mandatory dots (if any).
- If any condition fails, both paths reset to their starts.

Solution rule:
- Find a path that simultaneously guides both lines to their endpoints.
- Think about how each of your moves affects the mirror line.
